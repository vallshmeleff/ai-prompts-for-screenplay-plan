Dossier. What is known about Jett
Born in 1962 in the small town of XXXX.
At the age of one and a half months, he moved with his parents to a tiny village in Central Asia,
where a communications station supporting lunar exploration was based.
Then his father was assigned to the Cosmodrome, and the family moved again.
Jett lived in the City for 14 years.
After this time, Jett's father was assigned to the Rocket Corporation, and the family moved again.
There, Jett finished high school and entered the University, where he received professional training in Automatic Control Systems.
From this point on, the information about Jett begins to become ambiguous.
Some documents indicate that Jett had significant experience managing space missions. According to others, he was an officer in the Strategic Forces of Central Command.
It should be noted that this information is derived from paper documents and is currently impossible to verify.
Around this period, Jett's extraordinary analytical skills attracted the attention of the EC department. Jett was included in the Confidential Commission and has participated in several projects.
Moreover, Jett was not informed about the true goals of the projects.
All this data is still obtained from digitized documents. There are no originals.
Therefore, further discrepancies in the data intensify.
The following has been reliably established: the DNA samples for the entire period are identical.
